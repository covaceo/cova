Use case

Cova Practice lets a trader configure a paper account, choose a market/date/setup, replay historical candles, simulate Buy/Sell/Close decisions, and convert those practice reps into P&L, R-multiple, drawdown, rule-follow, setup scorecard, and live-permission analytics.

Compliance boundary

  • No live order routing.
  • No broker execution.
  • No customer fund movement.
  • Cova owns only replay, simulated execution, journaling, and analytics.

Why not the public widget?

  • Needs custom historical datafeed.
  • Needs replay-time control and execution marks.
  • Needs saved chart layouts/settings.
  • Needs premium in-app chart workstation UX.
